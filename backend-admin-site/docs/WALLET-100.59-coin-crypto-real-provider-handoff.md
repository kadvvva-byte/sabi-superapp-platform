# WALLET-100.59 — Coin/Crypto real provider handoff

This step prepares the handoff for real Coin and Crypto providers without enabling execution.

Scope:
- Coin Wallet ledger / treasury provider handoff.
- Crypto custody provider handoff.
- Crypto market data provider handoff.
- Token-only / provider-reference-only boundaries.
- No fake Coin movement.
- No fake crypto transaction.
- No fake market price.
- No Sabi storage of seed phrases or private keys.

Live readiness must remain false until real provider contracts, vault references, webhooks, adapters, and compliance approvals are connected.
