# WHOLESALE-MARKET-1.2 — Wholesale Market foundation

This module is the country-based wholesale market for Sabi.

Rules frozen in this foundation:

- User chooses a country / domestic market context.
- Each country has local wholesalers and products.
- Categories are safe local wholesale categories: food, clothing, textiles/materials, household goods, construction materials, packaging, tools, agro/farm-safe goods, business goods and safe electrical goods.
- Excluded from this module: chemicals, petroleum products, metals, gold/jewelry, precious stones, alcohol, tobacco, weapons, restricted goods and other compliance-heavy categories.
- Wholesalers may sell wholesale and retail where allowed.
- No fake orders, fake payments, fake delivery, fake merchant activation or fake verification.
- Buyer access requires AML/KYC later.
- Seller access requires Business Account, Business Merchant Account, KYB/KYC, Admin approval and compliance checks later.
- Payment route: Wallet / COIN / Business Merchant later.
- Communication route: Messenger.
- Promotion route: Business Stream.
- Location route: Sabi Map / Yandex layer.
- Profile route: shared Sabi Profile.

Technical route: `/wholesale-market`.
